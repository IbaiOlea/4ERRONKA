About Us

RevivaTech: Renewable technology, safeguarding the future.

RevivaTech was founded in 2023 with the aim of offering an innovative option in the second-hand computer device market. From the very beginning, our goal has been to extend the life of electronic devices and help reduce waste by promoting recycling and reuse.

In collaboration with the Sasieta community, we collect and repair old and unused devices and parts. Our experts meticulously examine each technology piece, performing necessary repairs or utilizing usable parts to recover other devices. Through our web platform, users can sell devices, receive evaluations, and make secure purchases sustainably.

Throughout our history, our commitment to innovation and responsibility towards the environment has guided us, and we will continue to offer sustainable and accessible technology in the future.

Principles

At RevivaTech, our principles are clear:

  • Sustainability: We promote the reduction of electronic waste through technology, aiming to protect the environment.
  • Quality: We rigorously assess the devices we repair, ensuring products arrive in excellent condition.
  • Transparency: We provide customers with clear and reliable information regarding the condition of products and their prices.
  • Environment and Reuse: By reusing and recycling devices, we help create a more responsible and sustainable society.

Company Operation

The operation of RevivaTech is clear and straightforward:

  1. Device Collection: Customers can request to sell devices through the website. After scheduling an appointment with one of our technicians, the device will be evaluated.
  2. Inspection: Once the device arrives at our warehouse, our experts assess its condition and perform necessary repairs.
  3. Resale: If the device is in good condition, it is added to our catalog and can be purchased by customers through the website.
  4. Recycling: We manage devices in poor condition by recovering usable parts and reusing components.

Warehouse Locations

RevivaTech has several warehouses across the Basque Country. The main warehouses are as follows:

  • San Sebastián: The Gipuzkoa warehouse, in collaboration with the Sasieta community, where devices intended for recycling are collected.
  • Bilbao: The Bizkaia warehouse, where we review products and add them to the resale catalog.
  • Vitoria: The Álava warehouse, where the main processes for reusing and repairing parts take place.
